**Healthmate** is an advanced, cross-platform healthcare ecosystem designed to digitize medical interactions between **Patients, Doctors, and Pharmacists**. It streamlines clinic management, reduces waiting times, and builds a trusted medical community.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 👨‍⚕️ Multi-Role Architecture
A unified app with three distinct interfaces tailored for:
- **Patients:** To book, track, and interact.
- **Doctors:** To manage clinics, schedules, and patient flow.
- **Pharmacists:** To share medical insights and manage digital interactions.

### ⏳ Live Queue Management
Real-time tracking system for clinic queues. Patients can see their turn live on the app, drastically reducing physical wait times and organizing clinic flow.

### 📅 Smart Appointment System
Seamless booking engine with instant synchronization. Doctors can confirm or reschedule, and patients receive real-time updates.

### 💊 Medication Compliance
Personalized reminder system that helps patients adhere to their treatment schedules through smart notifications.

### 🌐 Interactive Medical Community
A social feed where healthcare professionals share articles and advice. Features a modern UI for engagement, likes, and knowledge sharing.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture

- **Frontend:** High-performance HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.
- **Mobile Wrapper:** **Capacitor** (Native Bridge) compiled via Terminal & Android Studio for native performance.
- **Backend:** **Firebase** (Firestore for Real-time Sync, Auth for security, and Cloud Storage).
- **Design Pattern:** Feature-driven Clean Architecture—built for scalability and easy maintenance.
